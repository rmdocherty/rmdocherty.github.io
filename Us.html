Us

Ed-Aid (UK registered charity number: 1002499) is a charity based in Hertforshire and Kent, who's goal it is to provide children in Gambia with educational supplies so that they can fulfil their potential in life, by shipping educational equipment that our local schools no longer have any use for, and by funding renovation or rebuilding of classrooms, toilets or staff accomadation.

We've sent 15,00 boxes full of useful goods to the Gambia, which is then recieved by the Gambian Catholic Education Secretariat. Because of this, we always need more old school supplies and money in order to always provide for those less fortunate than ourselves. If you want to help, visit the donate section to give money or see where you can drop off anything you can, or even volunteer to pack boxes.
